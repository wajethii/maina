Portia's

Introduction

Portia's is a popular fashion brand that caters to men, women, and kids, offering a wide range of stylish and affordable clothing and accessories, from headwear to branded shoes. The company is looking to expand its digital presence by creating a user-friendly eCommerce website and mobile app to enhance the shopping experience for its customers.

Objective

To design and develop an eCommerce website and mobile app that delivers an exceptional user experience, effectively showcases Portia's extensive range of products, and drives online sales. The platform will be crafted to reflect the brand's commitment to style, affordability, and quality, offering a seamless shopping experience for men, women, and kids.

Research & Discovery

This comprehensive approach helped identify key insights across various demographics. Simultaneously, I analyzed competitors' websites and apps to identify industry best practices and potential areas where Portia's could differentiate itself in the market.

Additionally, I reviewed Portia's existing content, including product photos, descriptions, and branding materials, to determine which assets could be effectively repurposed for the new digital platform. This ensured that the new eCommerce website and mobile app would not only align with Portia's brand identity but also resonate with its diverse customer base

User testing

I conducted usability tests with a small group of participants to identify any usability issues and design flaws specific to Portia's eCommerce platform. Based on the feedback received, I iterated on the designs to improve the overall user experience. Additionally, A/B tests were implemented on various design elements and features to assess which variations performed better in terms of user engagement and conversions. This process ensured that the final design not only met the needs of Portia's diverse audience but also optimized the platform for higher sales and customer satisfaction.
