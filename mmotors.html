M-motors

motorcycle store

Introduction

M-Motors is a leading motorcycle store that specializes in sourcing high-quality motorcycles locally and importing them to meet the specific needs of its clients. The company is expanding its digital presence by developing a user-friendly eCommerce website and mobile app to streamline the purchasing process and enhance the overall shopping experience for motorcycle enthusiasts.

Objective

To design and develop an eCommerce website and mobile app for M-Motors that delivers an exceptional user experience, effectively showcases its range of locally sourced and imported motorcycles, and drives online sales. In a market with limited motorcycle dealerships, this platform will serve as a reliable and comprehensive online destination for motorcycle enthusiasts across Kenya.

The platform will feature intuitive navigation, detailed product listings, and secure payment options, ensuring a seamless shopping experience. By reflecting M-Motors' commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, the website and app will position the company as the leading online source for motorcycles in the region.

Research & Discovery

I conducted user interviews and surveys to gain a deep understanding of the preferences, needs, and challenges faced by M-Motors target audienceâ€”motorcycle buyers across Kenya. This thorough approach provided valuable insights into what potential customers are looking for. I also analyzed competitors' websites and apps to identify best practices and opportunities for M-Motors to stand out in the marketplace.

Furthermore, I reviewed M-Motors existing content, including product descriptions, visuals, and branding assets, to determine how they could be effectively utilized on the new digital platform.

User testing

I conducted usability tests with a small group to identify usability issues and design flaws specific to M-Motors' eCommerce platform. Based on this feedback, I refined the designs to enhance the overall user experience. Additionally, A/B testing was employed on various design elements to determine which versions performed best in terms of user engagement and conversions. This approach ensured that the final design effectively met the needs of motorcycle buyers and optimized the platform for increased sales and customer satisfaction.
